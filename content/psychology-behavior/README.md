## Overview

Focuses on relatable human experiences (e.g., procrastination, self-sabotage, and validation). High retention due to "curiosity loops." Older, more intentional audiences lead to higher engagement.

## Why This Niche?

- Universally relatable content
- Curiosity-driven viewing (people want to understand themselves)
- Mature audience = better ad engagement
